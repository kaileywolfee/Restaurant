The Rarebit is a retro-style restaurant and bar located in the heart of downtown Charleston. It is known for its upscale southern comfort food, ranging from chicken and waffles to patty melts. Stop by for Happy Hour - Tuesday through Friday from 4:00pm to 7:00pm to enjoy a Moscow Mule made with homemade ginger beer.
